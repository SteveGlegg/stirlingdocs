1. **Look at Drawings** to asses where paint is being applied
2. Place Piece **face down** on the sanding table
3. With the **ORBITAL sander** square to the face of the piece, sand all 4 edges with <Highlight color="#6B4B85">**150 grit**</Highlight> to remove machining marks
4. If smooth, sand all 4 edges with <Highlight color="#10A5EF">**320 grit**</Highlight> to polish
5. Ease all 4 corners to a 1.0mm profile with <Highlight color="#6B4B85">**150 grit**</Highlight>
6. Sand back face of piece with <Highlight color="#10A5EF">**320 grit**</Highlight>. **2 passes**
7. Run <Highlight color="#6B4B85">**150 grit**</Highlight> around the eased profile to even it out and knock off the sharp points
8. Make sure it is labelled, and that the label **will not be hidden** when painted
9. Flip piece to face side up
10. Sand face with <Highlight color="#10A5EF">**320 grit**</Highlight>, looking for **imperfections and glue**
11. Place on a spray cart or gently into a red cart for later.
